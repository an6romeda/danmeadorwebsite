About

DM

I grew up moving between states, spent my last six years of childhood in Germany, and somewhere along the way picked up the habit of making things. Model airplanes, music—a trebuchet.

I went to law school at night and on the weekends while working full time. A data breach led me to privacy law. Now, I help companies protect the people whose data they handle.

I'm still building, just with different tools (lately that's been Claude Code).
